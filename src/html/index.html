About Me

I am a former industrial engineer from Spain. I love computers since I was a child. And this love has led me to become a web developer.

In my early days as Java developer, I have been lucky to met an Agile developer who has introduced me in the world of Test Driven Development, Clean Code and SOLID principles. Thank you so much!

Right now, Learn.Modern Developer is helping me to become a Fullstack Developer. This portfolio has been developed with the knowledge acquired in the course.

In my spare time, I like to take a walk with my family. Once our little daughter is sleeping, my wife and I like to watch one of our favourite TV series. Lately, I enjoy playing old-school vintage arcade with friends.
