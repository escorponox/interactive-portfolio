About Me

I am a former industrial engineer from Spain. I love computers since I was a child. And this love has led me to become a web developer.

In my early days as Java developer, I have been lucky to met an Agile developer who has introduced me in the world of Test Driven Development, Clean Code and SOLID principles. Now I am test-first believer who loves adopting Agile best practices when coding. He probably does not remember me, but I must express my gratitude to that developer. Thank you so much Ignasi!

Right now, Learn.Modern Developer is helping me to become a Fullstack Developer. This portfolio, built without a single line of JavaScript, is a proof of how we can create pleasant user experiences by just mastering HTML and CSS, the basic frontend technologies.

In my spare time, I like to take a walk with my family. Once our little daughter is sleeping, my wife and I like to watch one of our favourite TV series. Lately, I enjoy playing old-school vintage arcade with some friends.
